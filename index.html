Sustainable Block Solutions
for California

California's 2026 CALGreen updates tighten embodied carbon, recycled content, and EPD requirements. Our machines and mix systems are built for full compliance from day one.

🌿 CALGreen 2026 Compliant Solutions

How We Support
CALGreen Compliance

♻️

Recycled Aggregate Mixes

Our machines run standard or recycled-content aggregates (RCA, crushed glass, fly ash). We support mix design documentation for CALGreen Section 4.408 compliance.

📋

EPD Documentation

Environmental Product Declarations for all block types produced with our equipment. Ready for LEED v4.1, CHPS, and municipal sustainability requirements.

🌡️

Embodied Carbon Reduction

On-site production eliminates transport emissions. Fly-ash and slag cement replacements reduce embodied carbon by up to 30% vs. standard OPC mixes.

📦

Material Conservation

Precise vibro-compression minimizes material waste per block. Consistent density and dimensions reduce rejection rates to under 2% on calibrated machines.

🏛️

Municipal Projects

We've supplied machines for Orange County public works projects requiring CALGreen and Buy Clean California Act compliance on hardscape and infrastructure.
