03 — Bittensor

The incentive layer that drives performance.

Bittensor is a decentralized network that uses economic incentives to produce digital commodities — including compute, bandwidth, and infrastructure. Miners (node operators) compete to provide the best service; validators score their performance; TAO rewards flow to those who deliver.

Block Machine harnesses this mechanism to forge self-optimizing RPC infrastructure. The network continuously rebalances toward the highest-performing operators without any centralized orchestration. Underperformance is economically penalized. Excellence is economically rewarded.

The result is infrastructure that gets better over time — not through manual maintenance cycles, but through the continuous pressure of market incentives operating at the protocol layer.
